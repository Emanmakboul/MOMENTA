##  Project Overview
A comprehensive maternal and child healthcare application operating under the supervision of the Ministry of Health. The platform provides integrated digital services to support mothers from pregnancy through early childhood.

Core Services Include:

Pregnancy tracking and fetal development monitoring.

Locating pediatric doctors across various specialties within the mother’s geographic area.

Vaccination tracking with automated reminders and identification of the nearest healthcare centers.

Child growth monitoring from birth, including normal developmental benchmarks and visual indicators of potential abnormalities.

Postpartum psychological support and educational resources for mothers.

AI-powered chatbot for instant guidance and assistance.

Personalized child nutrition recommendations.

Product recommendations tailored to the child’s needs, including nearby availability and simplified ordering options.

Premium services offering advanced features and enhanced support.
---

## Project Objectives
- To provide trusted, government-approved healthcare information for mothers and children from pregnancy through early childhood 
- To support maternal and child health monitoring by tracking pregnancy progress, child growth, and vaccination schedules
- To improve access to healthcare services by helping mothers locate nearby doctors, specialists, and care centers  
- To enhance maternal well-being through psychological support resources and educational content
- To deliver personalized digital services including AI assistance, nutrition guidance, and premium virtual consultations
